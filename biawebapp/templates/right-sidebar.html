Electricity Consumption : Exploratory Data Analysis

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is a crucial stage in comprehending energy use and generation across multiple industries and informing educated judgments. We can learn more about the variables that affect energy consumption, spot opportunities to increase energy efficiency, and make well-informed choices on energy policy, investment, and infrastructure development by examining patterns and trends in energy use and production.

It is impossible to overestimate the significance of energy generation and consumption, which is a vital component of environmental sustainability, technological innovation, and economic prosperity. A greater emphasis has been placed on renewable energy sources and energy efficiency in recent years as environmental concerns over the effects of energy usage have grown.

Understanding the patterns and trends in energy production and consumption across many industries, including transportation, commercial, industrial, and residential, is essential for addressing these issues. We can gain important insights into the sources and effectiveness of energy production and consumption, identify areas where improvements in energy efficiency can be made, and make well-informed decisions about energy policy and infrastructure planning by conducting an EDA of energy consumption and generation by sector. Ensuring a sustainable and successful future for our society will need understanding and tackling the difficulties of energy consumption and production, which can only be done by performing an EDA of energy generation and consumption by sector.
